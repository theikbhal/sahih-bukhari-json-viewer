Once a person asked Allah's Messenger () () about the night prayer. Allah's Apostle () replied, "The night prayer is offered as two Rak`at followed by two Rak`at and so on and if anyone is afraid of the approaching dawn (Fajr prayer) he should pray one Rak`ah and this will be a Witr for all the Rak`at which he has prayed before."
Hadith #90
